# Findings framework

This file is the place to summarize the most important findings after reviewing the outputs from `06_executive_queries.sql`.

Suggested structure:

## 1. Product growth
Identify the products with the largest AUM base and strongest AUM growth.

## 2. Flow leaders
Highlight which products are attracting the strongest net new money.

## 3. Performance leaders
Highlight which products are outperforming their benchmarks.

## 4. Commercial-performance disconnects
Note products with positive performance but negative flows, or positive flows despite weak excess returns.

## 5. Channel insights
Summarize which channels or regions carry the most AUM and where commercial concentration exists.

## 6. Retention insights
Summarize which client segments appear most stable and where retention risk may be building.
