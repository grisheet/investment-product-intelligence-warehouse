# Business case

A financial product organization needs more than performance reporting. It needs an analytics layer that connects commercial outcomes, product outcomes, and client behavior in one place.

This project simulates an internal product intelligence warehouse for an investment platform. The warehouse is designed to help leadership evaluate:

- Product growth through AUM trends.
- Commercial traction through subscriptions, redemptions, and net flows.
- Investment competitiveness through excess return versus benchmark.
- Client stability through retention behavior.
- Distribution strength through channel and regional analytics.

The project is intentionally structured as a reusable warehouse rather than a collection of one-off SQL queries. That makes it closer to how institutional product analytics teams actually work.
